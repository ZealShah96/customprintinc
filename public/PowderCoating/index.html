How Products are Powder Coated:
  • The powder coating is dry finishing process.
  • Finely ground particles of pigment and resin are electro statically charged and sprayed onto a part.
  • The parts to be coated are electrically grounded, so that the charged particles adhere to them until it goes into curing oven.
  • The curing cycle melts the powder, fusing it to the part.
  • The result is a uniform, attractive, durable, high quality finish.
  • The reject rate is minimal as there are no runs, drips, or sags.
